## Bias Check

### Perspectives Represented
- ✅ Official/institutional (Eurostat, OECD, EU Parliament)
- ✅ Academic/research (IZA, Springer, RIEDS)
- ✅ Business/employer (Deloitte, Tech.eu)
- ✅ Journalism/investigative (Fortune, Il Sole 24 ORE, Harvard Political Review)
- ⚠️ Worker/union perspective: Limited — ETUC document referenced but not deeply explored
- ⚠️ Youth voice: Only one direct quote from a young emigrant (Elena Picardi, src-004)

### Geographic Bias
- Sources are predominantly English-language, which may skew toward international/Northern perspectives on Italy
- SVIMEZ provides the key Southern perspective but is a single source for regional dynamics

**Status:** ⚠️ Moderate bias toward institutional/analytical framing. Could benefit from union reports, youth survey data, and Southern Italian media.

4. **Monitor** PNRR spending pace as the August 2026 deadline approaches — this will significantly affect Section 7 projections
